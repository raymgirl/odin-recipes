Pizza

Description

Pizza is a classic family favorite for parties and events. It's one of the easiest foods to make that allows you to feed as many people as possible. It's pretty darn cost efficient too. It's also undeniably helpful that pizza is absurdly delicious and fully customizable.

Pizza most commonly consists of a bread crust, sauce and cheese. Additional toppings may be added as desired. Below, we'll take a look at a good recipe for a traditional pizza. This can be used as a base recipe to create any type of pizza you would like.
