Tacos

Description

Tacos are easily one of the top two best foods on the entire planet. If you disagree, you might be from another planet!

In all seriousness though, tacos are a very easy, satisfying, and tasty meal to make. They are bsically just meat in a tortilla, and you customize them how you like them to be.

Below, I'll go over a good base recipe that you can follow to get your own tasty tacos.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas (Your choice of corn or flour)
  • Cooked Meat (Your choice of Beef, Chicken, or Pork)
  • Cilantro
  • Sliced Radishes
  • chopped Sweet Onion
  • Fresh Cut limes
  • Salsa or Hot Sauce (Optional)
  • Sliced Jalapenos (Optional)
  • Cheese of Your Choice (Optional)

Steps

  1. Cook your meat! Usually you will add seasoning or marinate your meat before cooking, however, this is optional.
  2. Once your meat is cooked you need to warm up your tortillas. Tortillas are usually steamed, but if you don't have the proper tools, you can warm them in the microwave for a few seconds.
  3. Add your cooked meat to your warm tortilla.
  4. Add the cilantro, onions, and radish slices to your taco. If you want, you can also add cheese and jalapenos right now.
  5. Squeeze a fresh lime over your taco. You want to get a good amount of lime juice all over the meat and other toppings. This provides a crucial flavor profile to your taco.
  6. At this point you can eat your taco as-is, or you can top it off with some of your favorite salsas or hot sauces.
  7. Eat and enjoy your amazing taco!
